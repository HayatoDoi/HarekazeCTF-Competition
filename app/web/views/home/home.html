Rules

  1. Harekaze CTF 2018 starts at 2018-02-10 06:00:00 (UTC) and ends at 2018-02-11 06:00:00 (UTC).
  2. Register one account per member.
    A team leader can make a team in the registration page.
    Other members can join the team by entering the team name and password in the registration page.
  3. There are no restrictions on the number of team members.
  4. The flag format is HarekazeCTF{example} unless otherwise stated.
  5. Violation of the rules may result in loss of points or disqualification.
  6. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us via our chat room.

Prohibited matter

  • To attack servers other than problem servers.
  • To attack other teams.
  • To share flags, solutions or hints with other teams.
  • To belong to multiple teams at the same time.
  • Other acts that impede management.
